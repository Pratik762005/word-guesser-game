Word Guessor Game

How it works

Description

Guess the hidden word letter by letter before you run out of attempts. Use hints, avoid mistakes, and challenge your vocabulary. Can you guess it in time?

Instructions

  1. Hints are provided when you tap Hints.
  2. Hints may include definition, antonyms, or synonyms.
  3. You have seven guesses.
  4. Each guess shows how many letters match the word (any position).
  5. It also shows how many letters are in the correct position.
  6. Press Enter when you finish typing a word.
  7. Lowercase counts; uppercase does not match.
  8. Press Enter again to lock in your guess and see the next row.
